Frequently Asked Questions

1. Where is this study room located?

2nd floor of CMPND Apartments, 666 Newark Ave, Jersey City, NJ 07306.

2. Cancellation policy

Cancel up to 2 hours before your start time for a full refund. Same-day cancellations convert to CMPND credits you can reuse within 30 days.

3. Operation hours

24 hours a day, 7 days a week. Your confirmation code grants access any time.

4. What if I stay overtime?

We automatically extend your booking in 30-minute increments at the same hourly rate, as long as the room is available. Need more time? Just tap your confirmation link to add hours.
